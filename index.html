About Us

D1 Fortification Private Limited has a guarding strength of over 3000 personnel, and has operations across the country. It provides security services to a large number of leading national and multinational companies. Under the D1 brand, it has become the most respected service provider. D1 Fortification has achieved such growth through commitment to the client, flexible management and excellent operation of it’s security services.

D1 follows rigorous procedures to ensure it’s core services and specialized services are of the best quality. Also, it has been certified under ISO 9001–2015. It offers comprehensive advice on every aspect of corporate security operations, commercial risk management, and personal security.
